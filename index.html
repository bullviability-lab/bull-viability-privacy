Privacy Policy

Last updated: 03.07.2026

BullViability ("the App") is an independent mobile application designed to help users create and manage cryptocurrency exit strategies. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how your information is handled.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects only the information necessary to provide its features and improve reliability.

  • Email address — used to identify your account and synchronize subscription status through RevenueCat.
  • Purchase information — subscription status and purchase entitlement are processed through Apple App Store, Google Play, and RevenueCat.
  • Product interaction data — collected through Firebase Analytics to understand general app usage and improve the product.
  • Crash data — collected through Firebase Crashlytics to diagnose crashes and improve app stability.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

BullViability does not collect or store:

  • cryptocurrency private keys;
  • wallet seed phrases;
  • wallet passwords;
  • exchange credentials;
  • bank account information;
  • payment card information;
  • government-issued identification documents.

Portfolio information remains stored locally on your device unless future backup features are explicitly enabled by you.

3. How We Use Information

Collected information is used only to:

  • provide access to BullViability Pro;
  • verify subscription status;
  • improve application stability;
  • understand anonymous usage patterns;
  • diagnose crashes and technical issues.

4. Third-Party Services

BullViability uses trusted third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — subscription management;
  • Google Firebase Analytics;
  • Firebase Crashlytics;
  • Firebase Remote Config;
  • CoinMarketCap API;
  • CoinGecko API, where applicable;
  • Binance API, where applicable.

These services process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

BullViability does not sell personal information. Personal information is shared only when necessary to provide app functionality through the third-party services listed above.

6. Data Security

Reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures are used to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account-related information by contacting: bullviability@gmail.com

Certain anonymous analytics or crash reports may not be individually removable because they do not contain personally identifiable information.

8. Children’s Privacy

BullViability is not intended for children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
